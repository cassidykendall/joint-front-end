Welcome to joint, the ultimate web application for two people managing shared finances.

Whether you’re just starting to merge budgets or have been splitting bills for years, joint simplifies the process of creating a joint budget and tracking income for an equitable division of expenses.

Developed by Cassidy Kendall, using a foundation created with Arkansas Coding Academy and with invaluable assistance from Software Developer Jake Davis, joint offers an intuitive solution to the often-complex world of shared finances. With joint, you’ll save time, reduce stress, and stay on the same page with your spouse, roommate, helpful parent — or whatever your situation is.

What is joint?

joint is an app designed to help you and your partner, friend, parent, or anyone else split up their joint spending activity.

What can you do with joint?

Keep track of specific expenses. Expenses can be searched by associate user, amount, or name. When new expenses are added to joint, you will be notified via email or SMS, your choice.
